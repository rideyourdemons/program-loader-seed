## Outbound Reading – Pressure, Control, and Men’s Health

The links below are for deeper reading. They are educational only and not affiliated with Ride Your Demons.

### Stress, pressure, and the body
- **Allostatic Load and Allostasis** – Overview of how chronic stress wears down the body over time, explaining why “pushing through” for years has real physical costs.  
  `https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK350265/`

- **Stress and the Heart** – Discussion of how long-term job strain, financial pressure, and emotional load increase risk for heart disease.  
  `https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4153910/`

### Work, control, and job strain
- **Work Stress and Cardiovascular Disease** – Review of how high demands and low control at work are linked with poorer heart health outcomes.  
  `https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4600094/`

### Men, coping, and help-seeking
- **Men, Masculinity, and Help-Seeking** – Exploration of how masculine norms shape when and how men seek support, and how this impacts mental and physical health.  
  `https://psycnet.apa.org/doi/10.1037/0003-066X.58.1.5`

### Decision-making under stress
- **The Effects of Stress on Cognitive Flexibility and Decision-Making** – Looks at how stress narrows thinking and pushes people toward short-term, reactive choices.  
  `https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2907136/`
