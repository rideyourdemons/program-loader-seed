How to Practice

Find a comfortable position. Inhale slowly through your nose for a count of four. Hold your breath for a count of four. Exhale slowly through your mouth for a count of four. Repeat this cycle several times.

This technique is based on research showing that controlled breathing can help activate the body's relaxation response. Many people find it helpful for managing everyday stress.
