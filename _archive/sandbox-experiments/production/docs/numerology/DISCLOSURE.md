# Numerology Disclosure

## Purpose

Numerology is presented on this platform as a **symbolic reflection tool only**, not as factual guidance, prediction, or decision-making support.

## What Numerology Is (On This Platform)

- An **optional** symbolic lens for reflection
- A metaphorical framing tool
- A way to explore themes and patterns symbolically

## What Numerology Is NOT (On This Platform)

- Factual truth or prediction
- Guidance or advice
- A decision-making tool
- A psychological assessment
- A therapeutic intervention
- A replacement for professional support

## How We Use Numerology

1. **Symbolic Overlay Only**: Numerology appears as an optional, symbolic overlay on tools and content
2. **Static Meanings**: All numerology meanings are fixed and documented - they do not adapt or change
3. **Non-Clinical**: Numerology meanings are never used for clinical, therapeutic, or diagnostic purposes
4. **Non-Prescriptive**: Numerology never tells you what to do, what will happen, or what you should believe
5. **Always Optional**: You can engage with numerology or ignore it - it does not affect tool functionality
