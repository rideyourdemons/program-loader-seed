Ethics & Boundaries

This platform is built to be practical, transparent, and non-clinical. The goal is to provide optional tools and reflection—not authority, pressure, or promises.

What we will not do

  • No diagnosis. We do not label you with conditions.
  • No treatment claims. We do not claim to heal, cure, or treat anything.
  • No emotional manipulation. No fear tactics, shame tactics, or urgency pressure.
  • No vulnerability targeting. We do not target people based on distress or personal sensitivity.
  • No coercive design. Tools are optional; navigation is not a trap.
  • No false authority. We do not claim supernatural certainty or guaranteed outcomes.

Commerce boundary

If the platform includes a store, merchandise is not positioned as necessary for progress. We do not present products as solutions to personal problems. The store is optional and separated from reflection content.

Numerology boundary

Numerology is treated as symbolic reflection only. It must never be presented as truth, prediction, or causation, and must never be used to steer decisions or purchases.

Transparency

The platform uses clear disclosures and keeps analytics in an observational role. The system is designed so that reflection content does not "turn into" persuasion or pressure.
