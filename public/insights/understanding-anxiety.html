Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, but when it becomes overwhelming, it can interfere with daily life.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is your body's natural response to perceived threats or stress. It's part of the "fight or flight" response that helps you react quickly to danger.

When Anxiety Becomes a Problem

While some anxiety is normal, it can become problematic when:
  • It's persistent and doesn't go away
  • It interferes with daily activities
  • It causes physical symptoms like rapid heartbeat or difficulty breathing
  • It leads to avoidance behaviors

    • Managing Anxiety

    There are many effective ways to manage anxiety, including:
  • Breathing exercises
  • Mindfulness practices
  • Cognitive reframing
  • Physical activity
  • Professional support when needed

Remember

Anxiety is treatable, and you're not alone. Many people experience anxiety, and there are tools and resources available to help.
